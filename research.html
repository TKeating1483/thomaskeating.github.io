THOMAS KEATING

Research

My research examines technology, speculative thinking, and long-term infrastructures, especially in relation to nuclear waste, hazardous futures, and environmental governance.

I am interested in how technical systems persist across vast temporal horizons, how abstraction shapes thought and practice, and how speculative approaches can open new ways of engaging with social, material, and ecological problems.

Themes in my work include nuclear waste futures, speculative geographies, philosophy of technology, technological infrastructures, and deep time.
