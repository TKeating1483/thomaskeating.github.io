Research

Research

Nuclear futures and deep time governance

Research on final repositories for highly radioactive waste, with attention to stewardship, communication, accountability, and the social imagination of futures extending tens of thousands of years.

Technology and social change

Work on technological logics, infrastructures, abstraction, and nonhuman processes, including ontogenetic approaches to technology in geography and adjacent fields.

Speculative thinking

Research engaging geography, philosophy, and critical theory to expand how empirical inquiry is understood, and to explore alternative ways of conceptualising social transformation.

Current projects

Current projects include research on accountability in nuclear waste repositories and work on civil society, scrutiny, and repository governance in Sweden.
