Thomas Keating

Assistant Professor Technology and Social Change (TEMAT) Linköping University

My research explores technology, speculative thinking, and the long-term futures of nuclear waste and infrastructures of deep time.

About

My work investigates how societies conceptualise and manage technological systems across extreme temporal scales. This includes the governance of radioactive waste, speculative approaches to technology, and philosophical questions concerning technological becoming.

Research

  • Nuclear waste futures and long-term governance
  • Speculative thinking and technological imaginaries
  • Philosophy of technology and ontogenesis
  • Technological infrastructures and deep time
